# MVP Milestones

## M1 - Governance and Product Baseline

**Outcome**: The repository has spec-kit, a ratified constitution, a baseline MVP spec, and GitHub-ready backlog templates.

**Epics**:
- E1 Spec and Governance Foundation

**Key deliverables**:
- `.specify` initialized
- Project constitution approved
- Initial spec, plan, and tasks artifacts approved
- Epic and user story issue templates available

## M2 - Identity and Tenant Foundation

**Outcome**: The platform has an approved identity and tenant model for Swedish-first onboarding and future provider expansion.

**Epics**:
- E2 Identity and Access Foundation

**Key deliverables**:
- BankID and Freja+ contracts documented
- User/person/role model agreed
- Future-provider extension strategy documented
- Identity compliance review started

## M3 - Attendance and LOK Automation

**Outcome**: Attendance becomes the only frontline club workflow needed for LOK-related administration.

**Epics**:
- E3 Attendance and LOK Automation

**Key deliverables**:
- Attendance workflow approved
- LOK evaluation model documented
- Export and submission lifecycle documented
- Audit and retry behavior defined

## M4 - Payments and Localization

**Outcome**: The MVP payment and language operations are fully specified for delivery.

**Epics**:
- E4 Payments and Reconciliation
- E5 Localization and Content Operations

**Key deliverables**:
- Swish and card payment journeys approved
- Internal payment lifecycle documented
- Swedish and English release gate defined
- Managed translation service shortlist approved

## M5 - MVP Build Readiness

**Outcome**: Approved specs are decomposed into implementation-ready work for the first build increments.

**Epics**:
- E2 Identity and Access Foundation
- E3 Attendance and LOK Automation
- E4 Payments and Reconciliation
- E5 Localization and Content Operations

**Key deliverables**:
- Priority user stories converted into implementation tasks
- Validation strategy mapped per story
- Operational and compliance sign-off criteria documented
